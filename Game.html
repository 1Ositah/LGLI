Love Game Love Idol is a free-to-play interactive dating simulation where your choices shape every moment of your story with rising idols on their journey to fame.
Step into a world where music, ambition, and romance collide. As you enter the lives of talented idols chasing their dreams, you’ll build meaningful relationships through immersive conversations, emotional events, and key decisions that determine how your story unfolds. Every interaction matters, what you say, how you respond, and the paths you choose will lead to multiple story routes and unique endings.
From lighthearted moments to deeply personal connections, your bond with each idol grows over time. Will you remain their biggest supporter, cheering from the sidelines? Or will your relationship evolve into something more intimate?

In Love Game Love Idol, you’re not just experiencing a story; you’re creating it.
Customize your favorite idols to match your style and vision. Choose from a variety of outfits and looks to express their personality or bring your own aesthetic to life. Whether you prefer a polished stage appearance or a more casual, off-duty vibe, your choices add another layer of connection and immersion to the experience.
As the idols grow in their careers, training, performing, and gaining recognition. You’ll be there for every milestone. Support them through challenges, celebrate their successes, and influence not only their personal journey but also their path in the spotlight.
The story doesn’t stop there. Enjoy special in-game events throughout the year that bring fresh content, exclusive interactions, and limited-time rewards. Celebrate romantic moments during Valentine’s Day, experience heartwarming scenes during Christmas, and discover unique storylines that can only be unlocked during these seasonal events. Each event offers new opportunities to deepen your relationships and create unforgettable memories.

Will you follow your heart, even if it complicates everything?
Will you prioritize their dreams, or your own feelings?
And when the spotlight shines the brightest… who will be standing beside you?
Your choices matter.
Your story is yours.
And your love story is waiting to begin.
