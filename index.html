NEW TALENT

MEI (芽衣)

A newly signed talent with raw potential and a spark that’s hard to ignore, she’s just beginning her journey in the idol world, but she already moves like she belongs on stage.
Specializing in dance and vocals, her performances carry a natural rhythm and emotional depth that hint at something bigger waiting to unfold. She may still be finding her place, but when the music starts, her confidence shines through in every step and every note.
Beyond performing, she quietly writes her own songs. Often inspired by passing thoughts, late-night feelings, and moments she doesn’t always know how to express out loud. Her lyrics are personal, honest, and sometimes a little unpredictable… just like her.
